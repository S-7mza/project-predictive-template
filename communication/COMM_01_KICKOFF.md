# Communication Plan – Workshop 1: Kickoff

## 1. Objectives
- Announce the project and create alignment.
- Confirm scope, roles, and high-level timeline.
- Capture initial risks, assumptions, and dependencies.

## 2. Stakeholder Groups
- **Primary:** Sponsor, Product Owner, Tech leads.
- **Secondary:** Key business stakeholders, BI/IT management.

## 3. Timeline & Touchpoints
- **T–5 to T–3 days:** Save-the-date + context.
- **T–2 days:** Detailed agenda + pre-work reminder.
- **T–0 morning:** "Starting today" reminder.
- **T+0 afternoon:** Thank you + minutes + action items.
- **T+2 days:** Follow-up on critical actions.

---
